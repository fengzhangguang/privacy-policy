Privacy Policy

This app (Silent Vibe Timer) does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app only uses the following system features:

  • Local notifications (for timer alerts)
  • Device vibration (for reminders)

All data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any servers.

This app does not require user registration or login.
